ABOUT US

Highlighting Variety and Hospitality Discover Essence Cafe Feilding,Feilding's award-winning café and bar where each visit is a taste of something special. Locally owned and operated, we add a personal touch to every aspect of our service—from our abundant breakfast, lunch, and dinner menus to our decadent desserts and signature drinks. Our dedication to quality is perhaps best showcased in our expertly crafted teas, brewed with care and precision to deliver exceptional flavor in every sip.

Essence Cafe Feilding is more than just a dining experience; we are a community hub that welcomes everyone. We happily cater to private events, accommodate families and children, and ensure that dietary needs are met with ease. Our commitment to excellence shines through not only in the food and drink we serve but also in the genuine hospitality that greets you the moment you arrive.

Visit Essence Cafe Feilding and savor a taste of true quality.
